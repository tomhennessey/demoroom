The University of Illinois' Chemistry Department's Demo Room is a place for chemistry lecturers to request demonstrations for their lectures. The team in the demo room consists of student workers from a variety of majors working together to prepare experiments from a catalogue of about 100 demonstrations. If you're currently an undergraduate at the University of Illinois and are interested in applying to work in the demo room, or another area of the chemistry teaching labs, please click here to apply.
